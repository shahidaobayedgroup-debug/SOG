About Us

Our Journey, Our Commitment

Who We Are

Our company's journey began in 2011 with real estate under Holy Dweling Limited. In 2013, we established Shahida Trade International, an export-oriented garments business. In 2018, we diversified, launching the Deshi brand of table salt with Shahida Salt Industries. We followed with Deshi edible oil in 2019 (Shahida Edible Oil Mills) and Mr. Clean toiletries in 2020 (Obayed Toiletries Limited), expanding our range of Deshi FMCG products. We are committed to providing Bangladeshi consumers with essential household items under trusted Deshi brands.

Our Core Principles

The foundation of our success and the guide for our future.

Our Mission

To provide Bangladeshi consumers with essential, high-quality household items under trusted local brands, enhancing their quality of life through excellence in manufacturing and service.

Our Vision

To be Bangladesh's most trusted and leading diversified conglomerate, synonymous with quality, reliability, and innovation, while expanding our footprint nationwide and contributing to the national economy.

Our Values

Quality: Uncompromising standards in every product.
Trust: Building lasting relationships with our stakeholders.
Integrity: Upholding ethical business practices.
Innovation: Continuously improving and evolving.
