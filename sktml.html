Weaving the Future of Textiles

Sukkor Ali Khan Textile Mills Limited (SKTML) is a cornerstone of the Shahida Obayed Group's diversified portfolio. Established with a vision to produce world-class textiles, SKTML combines state-of-the-art technology with a skilled workforce to deliver superior quality fabrics for the global market.
