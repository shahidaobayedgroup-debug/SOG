A Heart Bright with Hope

MESSAGE FROM THE CHAIRMAN

Mr. Golam Sarwar Saeed

Our company's journey began in 2011 with real estate under Holy Dweling Limited. In 2013, we established Shahida Trade International, an export-oriented garments business. In 2018, we diversified, launching the Deshi brand of table salt with Shahida Salt Industries. We followed with Deshi edible oil in 2019 (Shahida Edible Oil Mills) and Mr. Clean toiletries in 2020 (Obayed Toiletries Limited), expanding our range of Deshi FMCG products. We are committed to providing Bangladeshi consumers with essential household items under trusted Deshi brands.
